About Us

Rock Bottom Sapphires

Rock Bottom Sapphires is a proudly Australian owned gemstone business with over ten years of hands-on experience mining in the renowned Central Queensland Gemfields. Our journey began in the dirt and stone, where we developed a deep respect for the land and the natural beauty hidden beneath it. Over the years, we have worked extensively in both surface and underground mining operations, giving us a thorough understanding of sapphire formation and quality from the very source.

What truly sets Rock Bottom Sapphires apart is our ability to manage the entire process in-house. From mining the rough stone to expertly cutting and polishing each sapphire, we maintain full control over quality and craftsmanship at every stage. This allows us to deliver respectfully sourced, carefully finished sapphires that showcase their natural brilliance, ensuring every piece tells an authentic story.
